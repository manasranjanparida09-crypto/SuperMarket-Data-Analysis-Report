# 🛒 SuperMarket Data Analysis – Excel Dashboard
<img src="image_1.png" alt="Description of image" width="500">
## 📌 Overview
This project showcases a **Microsoft Excel dashboard** built for analyzing supermarket sales data.  
It highlights customer demographics, regional performance, and sales channels, providing actionable insights for marketing and sales growth.

---

## 📊 Features
- **Order vs Sales Trend** – Monthly comparison of orders and revenue.
- **Customer Demographics** – Gender split: Women (64%) vs Men (36%).
- **Order Status Tracking** – Delivered, Refunded, Returned, Cancelled.
- **Top Performing States** – Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh lead in sales.
- **Age vs Gender Analysis** – Adults (30–59 yrs) contribute the most.
- **Channel Performance** – Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra drive ~80% of orders.

---

## 📈 Insights
1. Women are more likely to purchase compared to men.
2. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top contributing states.
3. Adults (30–59 yrs) form the primary customer base.
4. Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra dominate sales channels.

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
To improve supermarket sales, **target women aged 30–49 in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** with ads, coupons, and promotions on **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra**.
